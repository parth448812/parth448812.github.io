Project Design

Overview

Description:

This website is for my client Kashaypkumar Patel, who has a passionate interest in Luxury Watches and their affordable alternatives. The purpose of this webpage is to showcase that passion to the world.

Intended Users:

The intended users of this website are going to be everyone who are interested in learning about luxury watches. Mainly the users will be the people who are interested in either purchasing luxury watches and have come to the site to enhance their knowledge or people looking for affordable alternatives.

Content Overview:

Overall, this website will teach the users about luxury watches and their affordable alternatives. It will also promote watch brands such as Audemars Piguet, Rolex, Patek Phillipe, Orient, Tissot, etc. One of the goal of this website is to potentially help users discover a passion they might have for watches aswell.
