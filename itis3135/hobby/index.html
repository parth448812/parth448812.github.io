Where Do You Box?

The best places to box would be a boxing gym, but a normal gym would also work. What makes a boxing gym unique to a normal gym would be the equipment such as heavy bags, reflex bags, boxing rings, etc.

Why Should You Start Boxing?

  1. It a great form of exercise because it works the entire body, primarily the arms.
  2. It is also a good way to keep your brain occupied because it requires a lot of thinking. With every punch you throw, the way your feet and hands are placed, it requireds you to think about your next move, while thinking about your opponents in order to counter.
  3. Boxing also allows you to protect yourself IF you are in a scenario where no other option is available.

How Do You Start Training Boxing?

  1. Cardio: Running improves stamina, which allows you to last longer in a fight and not get exhausted.
  2. Shadowboxing: Imagining an opponent in front of you to practice footwork, hand placement, and defensive manuvears.
  3. Resistence Bands/Weight Lifting: Increases handspeed and power.
  4. Sparring: Practice your Ring IQ, get comfortable being hit, test out different techniques. Nothing beats sparring because it is the closet you will get to a fight without being in a fight.
