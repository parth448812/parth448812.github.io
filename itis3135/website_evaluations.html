Website Evaluations

Worst Website

This is a horrible website becasue it does not comply with any of the principles of CRAP.

  • C - Contrast: Reading the elements in the site is extremely difficult as the site has too many varying neon colors on a white background. This sharp contrast makes it very hard to focus on one single element.
  • R - Repetition: This site has over a 100 commercials with the same format mushed together, making it annoying and boring.
  • A - Alignment: Everything is out of alignment as the commericial sections are in no proper
    space apart just randomly put on as the inside has text in no specific order.
  • P - Proximity: There are so many commericals on the page with little to no space in between
    just split by a black border and some without.

Good Website

On the contrary, this is a website that follows the principles of CRAP.

  • C - Contrast: Audemars Piguet store uses a simple black background with white text but
    colorful images and videos. It even uses unique textured backgrounds when showcasing their unique watches.
  • R - Repetition: The site does not repeat elements as each of the elements are represents a differnt watch and style. This makes each watch represented in the best way possible.
  • A - Alignment: Audemars Pigquet uses a variety of sections that are each aligned wonderfully with an image. Each of these setions has the text large with unique fonts and captions.
  • P - Proximity: As you scroll down the site, you will see how the text is large, but adjacent to an well-placed image. This format is repreated but the image and text is flipped by each section and watch, making the site engaging.
